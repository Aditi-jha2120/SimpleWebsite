1. Online College Canteen Ordering System 

Purpose: Designed to simplify and streamline the process of ordering food from a college canteen.
Menu Browsing: Students can view available food options and their prices online.
            
Order Placement: Users can place orders directly through the platform without queuing in the canteen.
                Payment Integration: Added secure payment gateways for online transactions.
Order Management: Includes a system for tracking and managing incoming orders for canteen staff.

2. 7 Wonders of the world 

Purpose: Built a visually appealing and informational website dedicated to showcasing the 7 Wonders of the World.
Informational Pages: Detailed descriptions, history, and images of each wonder.
            
Interactive Design: Integrated animations and interactive elements to enhance user engagement.
                Navigation: Smooth and intuitive navigation for accessing different sections.
Tools/Technologies Used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Photoshop (for image optimization).
Impact: Demonstrated proficiency in creating aesthetically pleasing designs and responsive web layouts.

3. 3. Bold Makeup Online Website 

Purpose: Developed an e-commerce platform for a makeup brand to sell their products online.
            
Product Display: Categorized sections for makeup items like lipsticks, foundations, and accessories.
            
Shopping Cart: Users could add products to their cart and proceed to checkout.
                Discount Management: Included dynamic offers and promotions for customers.
                Responsive Design: Ensured compatibility with mobile devices.
                Tools/Technologies Used: WordPress, WooCommerce, HTML, CSS, Photoshop.
                Impact: Helped the brand establish an online presence and attract a larger customer base.
